IJCNLP-AACL 2025 Overview

All deadlines follow the 11:59PM UTC-12:00 (“Anywhere on Earth”) time zone convention.


IJCNLP-AACL 2025 will be held in Mumbai, India. The main conference is scheduled for December 20–22, with tutorials, workshops, and demos taking place on December 23–24, 2025.

Call for Main Conference Papers

IJCNLP-AACL 2025 invites long and short papers on original research in Computational Linguistics and NLP. Topics include multilinguality, reasoning, fairness, generation, and more. Selected papers may also appear in TACL, CL, or Findings of ACL.

  • Two-stage review via ACL Rolling Review (ARR)
  • Submission via OpenReview
  • Special Theme: Chain-of-Thought Reasoning
  • Both in-person and virtual presentation options

Important Dates:

  • ARR Deadline: July 28, 2025
  • Commitment Deadline: October 10, 2025
  • Notification: October 25, 2025
  • Conference: December 20–22, 2025

Call for Workshops

IJCNLP-AACL 2025 invites proposals for workshops in NLP and related fields. Workshops will be held in Mumbai, India, and are encouraged to be in-person. Topics may span computational linguistics, multimodal processing, and more.

  • Proposals due: April 15, 2025
  • Notification of acceptance: May 8, 2025
  • Workshop dates: December 23–24, 2025
  • Encouraged: Diversity and Inclusion

Workshop proposals must be no more than four pages, covering the topic, format, organizers, and logistics. See the Workshop Proposal Template for more details.

Call for Tutorials

IJCNLP-AACL 2025 invites tutorial proposals in NLP and related fields, including non-CL topics of interest to the community. Early-career researchers are especially encouraged to submit.

  • Proposal Deadline: August 14, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: October 2, 2025
  • Tutorial Date: December 23, 2025

We welcome cutting-edge, foundational, or interdisciplinary tutorials. Diversity in content, representation, and outreach is strongly encouraged. Tutorials should be 3–4 hours long (half-day) or justified for full-day sessions.

Call for System Demonstrations

The IJCNLP-AACL 2025 Demo Track invites submissions of NLP systems—from research prototypes to production-ready tools. Open-source and industrial demos showing innovation are especially encouraged.

  • Submission Deadline: September 15, 2025
  • Notification: November 10, 2025
  • Camera Ready: November 24, 2025
  • Main Conference: December 20–24, 2025

Accepted demo papers will be published in a companion volume and must be presented live at the conference. Each paper must be accompanied by a demo video and a live URL or installable package link.
