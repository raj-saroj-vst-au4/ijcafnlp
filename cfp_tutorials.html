IJCNLP-AACL 2025 Tutorials

Learn from experts through comprehensive tutorials covering cutting-edge advances and foundational concepts in NLP and computational linguistics

Proposal Due: August 14, 2025
Notification: October 2, 2025
Duration: Half-day (3-4 hours)
Format: In-person preferred

Call for Tutorial Proposals

IJCNLP-AACL 2025 invites proposals for tutorials in all areas of natural language processing, computational linguistics, and related fields of interest to our communities.

Early Career Focus

We especially encourage submissions from early-career researchers to share their expertise and contribute to knowledge dissemination.

Tutorial Day

Tutorials will be held on December 23, 2025 in Mumbai, India, following the main conference days.

Presenter Requirement

For in-person tutorials, at least one organizer must be physically present at IJCNLP-AACL 2025.

Choose the format that best fits your content and audience

Diversity & Evaluation

Promoting diversity and evaluating tutorial quality across multiple dimensions

Diversity Considerations

Academic Diversity

Multilingual data, method scalability, resource accessibility, underrepresented communities

Representation

Diverse speakers, organizers, and program committee members from underrepresented groups

Participation

Mentoring, subsidies, affinity group coordination, diverse paper selection

Evaluation Criteria

Originality and impact of tutorial content
Expected interest level of participants
Quality of organizing team and expertise
Contribution to conference diversity
Educational value and practical applicability
