About IJCNLP 2025

The International Joint Conference on Natural Language Processing (IJCNLP) is a leading forum for research in Natural Language Processing. The conference brings together researchers and industry practitioners to share the latest advances in the field.

In 2025, IJCNLP will be held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, featuring keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, tutorials, workshops, and industry tracks focusing on cutting-edge NLP research and applications.

Call for Papers

Conference Papers

Overview

IJCNLP-AACL 2025 invites papers on substantial, original, and unpublished research in all aspects of Natural Language Processing.

Submission Topics

  • Machine Learning for NLP
  • Information Extraction
  • Machine Translation
  • Dialog and Interactive Systems
  • Language Generation
  • Multimodal NLP

Two Stage Review Process

1. Submit to ARR (Action Editor assigns reviewers)

2. Commit accepted papers to IJCNLP-AACL 2025

Submission Details

  • Long papers: Up to 8 pages + references
  • Short papers: Up to 4 pages + references
  • Unlimited pages for appendices
  • Blind review process

Contact

papers@ijcnlp2025.org

Workshop Papers

Overview

We welcome proposals for workshops on specialized topics in NLP. Workshops can be half-day or full-day events.

Workshop Types

  • Regular Workshops
  • Theme Workshops
  • Shared Task Workshops

Important Dates

  • Proposal Due: April 30, 2025
  • Notification: May 21, 2025
  • Workshop: December 20, 2025

Submission Requirements

  • Workshop description (2 pages)
  • Organizing committee details
  • Expected number of participants
  • Previous editions (if any)

Contact

workshops@ijcnlp2025.org

System Demonstrations

Overview

The system demonstration track showcases state-of-the-art language processing technology through live demonstrations.

Submission Categories

  • Research Prototypes
  • Industrial Applications
  • Open-Source Tools
  • Educational Systems

Requirements

  • Paper (6 pages + references)
  • Screencast video (5-10 minutes)
  • Live demo at conference

Review Process

Papers will be reviewed based on technical merit, novelty, relevance, and potential impact.

Contact

demos@ijcnlp2025.org

General Submission Guidelines

Mandatory Reviewing Workload

  • Primary authors must review 2 papers
  • Secondary authors must review 1 paper
  • Reviews due within 3 weeks of assignment
  • Quality checks will be performed

Presentation at the Conference

  • At least one author must register
  • Presentation format will be hybrid
  • Presentation slides due 1 week before
  • Q&A session mandatory
