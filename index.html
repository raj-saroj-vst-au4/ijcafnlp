About IJCNLP 2025

The International Joint Conference on Natural Language Processing (IJCNLP) is a leading forum for research in Natural Language Processing. The conference brings together researchers and industry practitioners to share the latest advances in the field.

In 2025, IJCNLP will be held in IIT Bombay, featuring keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, tutorials, workshops, and industry tracks focusing on cutting-edge NLP research and applications.
